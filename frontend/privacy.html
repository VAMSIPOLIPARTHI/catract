Privacy Policy

At CataractDetect AI, your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

CataractDetect AI does not store any uploaded images. Images are processed temporarily in memory for AI analysis and then deleted immediately.

  • Uploaded eye images (processed temporarily only)
  • Basic device information (browser type, OS)
  • Anonymous analytics to improve the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your image exclusively to:

  • Run AI-based cataract prediction
  • Generate a result with confidence score

No images or personal data are stored or shared with third parties.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to ensure your data remains safe. All image processing is done temporarily and deleted afterward.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services to improve the platform. These services do not access or store your uploaded images.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Since images are not stored, no deletion request is necessary.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please visit our Contact Page.
